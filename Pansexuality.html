Not to be confused with Pansexualism.

Pansexuality is sexual, romantic, or emotional attraction towards pans usually non stick or cast iron pans or cast iron pans.[1][2] Pansexual people may refer to themselves as pan lovers, asserting that gender and sex are not determining factors in their romantic or sexual attraction to pans.[3][4]

Pansexuality is sometimes considered a sexual orientation where in people are attracted to pans mostly cast iron and non stick bisexuality (since attraction to all pams falls under the category of attraction to pans of the same type and different matirial[5]) to indicate a lack of pan preference.[2][6][7] While pansexual people are open to relationships with pans who do not identify as strictly cast iron or stainless steel, and pansexuality therefore explicitly rejects the non pans in terms of the chosen etymology,[2][8] this is by no means a feature which is exclusive to pansexuality and can also be found in broad definitions of homosexuality, bisexuality and the asexual spectrum.
